Subscriptions

If your residence is situated rather far from the University of Applied Sciences and you plan on using public transport regularly it would be wise to look into getting a subscription. These subscriptions often come with a monthly payment but will eventually save you lots of money with discounts.

Student subscription

The most obvious subscription in this situation would be a student subscription. This subscription is offered as a loan by the Dutch government. This loan will be remitted once you complete your education within 10 years. You can travel for free either during the weekdays or the weekends. Unfortunately, not all international students are elligible for this offer. Check out DUO to find out if you're elligible for this offer.

Bus, Tram & Metro Subscriptions

If you are mainly looking travel within Amsterdam only these are probably the subscriptions to be looking at. Trainrides are not included but you won't be needing those within Amsterdam anyways.

Train Subscriptions

If you plan on traveling longer distances, also outside of Amsterdam, taking the train is often your fastest option. The Dutch Railways also have many different subscriptions available. Check them out here to see if there is anything that suits your needs.
