General

Public Transport is a very important asset to any students in the Netherlands, especially in a big city like Amsterdam.
Therefor it is recommended to get to know the Dutch Public Transport system rather quickly. This app will guide you with this.

If you've just arrived on Schiphol airport for the first time and are a bit lost, head on over to the Schiphol tab, which has information on the locations from where you can take a train or bus.

If you're simply wanting to get home to your appartment quickly, select the Tickets tab to find out how you are able to buy a ticket for a ride home.

If you're planning on using the Dutch public transport often (which you probably should) it will be beneficial to take a look into getting a subscription. The information regarding this can be found under the Subscriptions tab.
