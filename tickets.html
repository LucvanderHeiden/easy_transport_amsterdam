Tickets

Before travelling with public transport you need one very crucial thing, a ticket. Tickets can be aquired in multiple different ways.

Online Tickets

One way is to buy a ticket online. A ticket can be bought online at a slight discount here saving you not only stress but also money.

Physical Tickets

Another way is to buy physical tickets on location. Trainstations have terminals where you can buy a ticket for you trip. These terminals are bright yellow with the text "Train Tickets" on the top.

If you're traveling with the bus or subway you can buy a ticket inside. This method works if you're in a hurry but do mind that this option is more expensive than buying a ticket online.
